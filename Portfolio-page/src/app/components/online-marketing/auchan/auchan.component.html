Auchan PPC Management

I worked more than 1 year as the key member of HPS’s Auchan PPC team. Our task was to develop PPC strategy, manage Google Ads and Facebook campaigns on a daily basis. We were also responsible for analyzing, maintaining, and optimizing Auchan Magyarország’s Google Analytics accounts.


Auchan started its online home delivery service back in 2016 when we started to work together. It belonged to my daily tasks to manage online campaign for both the online- and Auchan’s physical stores. Besides planning digital media for various small and medium size campaigns (around 2-3 campaigns a week) I myself wrote the ad text for the ads.
