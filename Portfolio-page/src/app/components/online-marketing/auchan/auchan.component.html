LeasePlan PPC Management

LeasePlan is a leading multinational leasing company. My task is to generate as many leads from the SME sector as possible. In order to do this, we drive highly relevant, paid traffic to LeasePlan’s website from different online channels like Google Search, Display Display Network, YouTube, Facebook and LinkedIn.

We created a digital lead generating strategy that is made up of different steps such as user education step followed by direct product offer ads. Thus we maintain several sophisticated remarketing lists that enable us to show our target audience the most attractive offer based on the user’s previous behavior on the website.

We take the advantage of using the latest ad types provided. For example, carefully designed Facebook Canvas and Collection ads. Furthermore we enhance LeasePlan’s online campaigns with dynamic remarketing both on Facebook/Instagram and Google Display Network.


Besides managing LeasePlan's ad accounts sometimes I design ad creatives in Photoshop and write ad copy.
